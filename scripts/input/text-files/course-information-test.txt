
Course Information Guide

Prerequisites:
Students must complete prerequisite courses before enrolling in advanced classes.

Course Scheduling:
- Fall semester: August - December
- Spring semester: January - May  
- Summer sessions: June - July

Course Load:
- Full-time: 12+ credit hours
- Part-time: Less than 12 credit hours
- Maximum: 18 credit hours per semester

Program Requirements:
Each degree program has specific course requirements outlined in the catalog.
