
Frequently Asked Questions

Q: How do I register for classes?
A: Use the online student portal during your registration window.

Q: What is the deadline for tuition payment?
A: Tuition is due by the first day of classes each semester.

Q: How do I apply for financial aid?
A: Complete the FAFSA by March 1st for priority consideration.

Q: Where can I get academic advising?
A: Contact your department advisor or visit the Academic Success Center.
